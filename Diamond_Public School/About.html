About Us

Diamond Public School (DPS) is committed to providing quality English-medium, technology-driven education, addressing two of the biggest challenges many parents face—accessibility and affordability. In many small towns and semi-urban areas, parents often struggle to offer their children the education they deserve due to these constraints. DPS, through its strategic school.

Many parents, due to constraints of Accessibility and Affordability, fail to provide their children the much deserved quality education. DPS thus makes the pedestal for every such parent in fulfilling their most important part of parenting responsibility. By establishing schools in small towns and semi-urban areas, DPS has become a trail blazing pioneer of quality education.

What’s next? The more and the best is yet to come!

Vision

Diamond Public School envisions playing a pivotal role in India's progress by making quality education both affordable and accessible to all. By breaking down barriers of cost and location, DPS aims to contribute to the creation of a more educated and empowered nation.

Mission

Diamond Public School is dedicated to serving students from all backgrounds, providing them with a safe and supportive environment where they can thrive. The school fosters collaboration among teachers, parents, and students, working together to nurture self-disciplined, responsible individuals.

MILESTONES

In the very first academic year 2014-15, 29 schools were launched in 4 states: Andhra Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh.

Within a short span of two years, DIS registered its mark in the Indian education industry with a flag growth of 35 schools in 6 states: Andhra Pradesh, Odisha, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, and West Bengal.
